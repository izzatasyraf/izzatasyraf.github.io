Project

FINAL YEAR PROJECT
IOT Smart Gardening

Smart Gardening system is an automated
water irrigation system that can be
monitored and controlled using smartphone.
User will also be notified when their plant
needs be watered and can be
watered through this system.

MOISTURE GRAPH

The moisture level can also be monitored and tracked in every 30 minutes or 3 hours. This graph will show change in moisture level of the plant.

MOISTURE LEVEL

The moisture level of your plant can be monitored in real time. The moisture level can be set to suit each different type of plant.


POP-UP NOTIFICATION

Pop-up notification will appear and user will be
notifeid when the moisture level is low and the plant needs water.

WHY THIS TOPIC WAS CHOSEN.

The reason behind developing automated garden watering system is to help people take care of their plants without having to interact with them daily. Thus, it will reduce physical human interface without affecting the growth of the plant.

HOW IT RELATES WITH PERSONAL GOALS.

My personal goal is to free my time but at the same time I don't have to worry about my plants so that I can focus with other things that is more important. This project helps me in being more productive and focus on my studies at the same time.

WHAT HAS BEEN LEARNT.

I have learnt that planning is very important. If the plan is good, the execution will be easier because we can already see what we need to achieve and how it has to be done. Having multiple plans also helps when our main plan does not went well.
